Insert your ATM Card in the ATM machine in the slot as marked in the above diagram.

Select your language from the language options appearing on the display screen.

Use the Keypad to enter your 4-digit ATM Pin Number. Do not share your ATM Pin with anyone.

On the ATM screen, select the type of transaction. For cash withdrawal, choose the Withdrawal Option.

After selecting the cash withdrawal option, choose your account type (e.g., savings account).

Enter the withdrawal amount. Make sure not to exceed your account balance.

Collect the cash from the machine.

You can choose to get a printed receipt for the transaction if needed.

If you want to undertake another transaction, you can select that option.
